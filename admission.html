Admission Information

Welcome to Our School

We are delighted that you are considering our school for your child's education. Here is some information about our admission process:

Admission Criteria

  • Age eligibility: Children must be between 3 and 5 years old for preschool, 6 and 10 years old for elementary, etc.
  • Submission of completed application form
  • Interview with parents and student (if applicable)

Documents Required

  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate of the child
  • Proof of address

Application Process

  1. Download the application form from our website.
  2. Complete the form and gather the required documents.
  3. Submit the application form and documents to the school office.
  4. Attend the scheduled interview (if applicable).
  5. Receive notification of admission status.
